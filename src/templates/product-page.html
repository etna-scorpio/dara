Acerola cherry is one of the most nutritionally dense superfruits with one of the highest levels of vitamin C and antioxidants of any food source.

Our acerola cherry powder is made from carefully harvested, wildcrafted organic acerola cherries. The acerola pulp and vitamin C rich skins are frozen immediately after harvesting in Brazil and then freeze-dried in the USA. The freeze-drying process is optimal for retaining the fruits potent nutrients. Our acerola cherry powder is the highest quality and purest powder available and is free of maltodextrin or other fillers.
